Carbonara Recipe

Description:

is a pasta dish made with fatty cured pork, hard cheese, eggs, salt, and black pepper. t is typical of the Lazio region of Italy. The dish took its modern form and name in the middle of the 20th century.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti (or any long pasta)
  • Eggs (usually 2 whole + 1 yolk per serving)
  • Pancetta or guanciale (Italian cured pork — can substitute with bacon if needed)
  • Pecorino Romano cheese (grated)
  • Parmesan cheese (optional, for milder flavor)
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt (for pasta water only — the cheese and pancetta are already salty)
