Lasagna Recipe

Description:

Lasagna, also known by the plural form lasagne, is a type of pasta made in wide, flat sheets. It originates in Italian cuisine, is best known for its use in a baked dish made by stacking layers of pasta, alternating with fillings such as ragù, béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef and Italian sausage
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Cottage cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella
  • Crushed tomato and Tomato paste
  • Seasoning(Oregano, Basil, Salt)

Steps:

  1. Brown the beef and onion until cooked through.
  2. Prepare the baking dish and mix the meat with sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, ricotta, meat sauce, and mozzarella; repeat layers.
  4. Bake covered for 1 hour or until noodles are tender.
  5. Uncover, add remaining mozzarella, and bake until golden.
  6. Cool for a few minutes before serving.
