Pizza Recipe

Description:

This homemade pizza features a crisp golden crust, rich tomato sauce, and gooey melted mozzarella. It's simple to make and tastes just like your favorite pizzeria version — perfect for any night of the week.

Ingredients:

Dough

  • All-purpose flour
  • Wark water
  • Active dry yeast
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • A pinch of sugar

The sauce

  • Crushed tomatoes or tomato passata
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (minced or powder)
  • Dried oregano or basil
  • Salt and pepper

The toppings

  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil drizzle (optional)
  • Pepperoni slices or other toppings (like mushrooms, bell peppers, or onions)

Steps:

  1. Activate yeast: Mix warm water with yeast and a pinch of sugar. Let it foam 5-10 min.
  2. Make dough: Add flour, salt, and a splash of olive oil. Mix, then knead until smooth and slightly tacky (7-10 min).
  3. First rise: Place in a lightly oiled bowl, cover, and let double in size (about 60-90 min)
  4. Make simple sauce: Stir crushed tomatoes with a little olive oil, minced garlic, oregano/basil, salt, and pepper. (No cooking needed.)
  5. Preheat oven hot: 250-260 °C / 475-500 °F. If you have a stone/steel, preheat it too; otherwise use an upside-down baking sheet.
  6. Shape dough Punch down, divide (for 1-2 pies), rest 10 min, then stretch by hand to a thin round with a slightly thicker rim.
  7. Assemble Lightly sauce (thin layer), add mozzarella, then toppings. Finish with a small olive-oil drizzle.
  8. Bake Slide onto the hot stone/sheet; bake 8-12 min until crust is browned and cheese is bubbling.
  9. Finish Cool 2-3 min, add fresh basil, slice, and serve.
